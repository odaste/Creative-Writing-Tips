Story Structure

The structure is important when you are creating a story. The structure you choose will have to be decided based on several aspects; what genre is it? What kind of story is it? Is it better having a chronological narrative or is it better starting right in the midst of the action? What would your readers prefer?


What we mainly think of when talking about structure, would probably be plot. There are, however, other aspects to structure, too.
